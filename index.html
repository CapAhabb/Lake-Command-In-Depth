Lake Command

AI-assisted environmental intelligence for lake conditions, species signals, dynamic overlays, and scenario planning.

Modular architecture Interactive mapping AI-assisted development Faux datasets Scenario planning Agent orchestration

What It Does

Turns weather, lake, depth, route, and species signals into a chartplotter-style command center for planning and on-water decisions.

Core Features

  • GIS mapping and dynamic overlays
  • Predictive analytics and trip signals
  • AI-agent roadmap for scenario planning

Stack Direction

Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, OpenLayers, GitHub, and AI-assisted development. Current prototype lives in Flutter.

Built as a showcase-ready prototype for environmental intelligence workflows.
